Welcome to the Wasteland Github

Wasteland is an In-Development 3D MMORPG set at a post-apocalyptic time.

What's Planned

  • Factions - Create factions with friends and or AI survivors, go to war with other factions, and manage faction finances.
  • Skills - Train your skills like Mechanics, Farming, Weaponcrafting, Armorcrafting, Science, and more.
  • AI - Survivor AI that can be either friendly or hostile to other survivors as well as enemy AI's that drop loot based on the rarity of the items.
  • Weapons / Combat - 3rd Person & First Person camera views for combat and multiple weapon varieties like Rifles, Pistols, Shotguns, and other things like Mounted and Static weapons.
  • Player Interaction - Add friends, Trade, Fight, Take hostages, etc.
